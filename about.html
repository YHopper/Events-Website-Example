History and Mission

Established in 1985, our venue was created out of the desire for a natural gettaway after our founder Harold Smith, tech entrepreneur and nature enthusiast, sought to find a vacation spot away from work. After inviting both family and friends to the venue for several years, Harold had the idea to expand the venue as a business to accomodate the general public.
Our mission is to provide you, your family and friends with exceptional service and top quality accomadations in order to create everlasting memories here in the Ouachita Mountains. Let us be your home away from home.

Harold Smith, Founder
